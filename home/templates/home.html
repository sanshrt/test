About.

SHACKLES 2025 is one of the annual state level technical symposium conducted by Mechasso.It has been held every year over a period of two days during either the month of February or March.Many participants participate in the symposium from various colleges across the state. It is conducted b Mechasso, Alagappa Chettiar Government College of Engineering & Technology. ACGCET-Karaikudi is an government autonomous college of engineering in Karaikudi, Tamilnadu, India. Established in 1952, it is under the control of the government of the State. The college is a member institute under the TEQIP - Technical Education Quality Improvement Programme in India (11 colleges were selected from Tamil Nadu).
