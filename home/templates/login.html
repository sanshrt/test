About.

Alagappa Chettiar College of Engineering and Technology (ACCET) is a prestigious engineering institution located in Karaikudi, Tamil Nadu. Founded in **1952**, the college is affiliated with **Anna University** and has consistently maintained a reputation for excellence in technical education and research. r its **state-of-the-art laboratories, industry collaborations, and student-driven projects**, making it a hub for aspiring mechanical engineers. It regularly organizes **workshops, guest lectures, and technical events** to keep students updated with the latest industry trends.
